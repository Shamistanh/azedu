Our Project

Little Introduction

The app will offer a list of classes, and it shall organize classes by categories like math, language, literature, and physics. Also, it allows its users to search information about the course description. All the materials uploaded to app will be divided into sections and the students can benefit those materials with free of charge. Beside of that, our app will preserve the right of the students and teachers. In case of inappropriate behavior, the user immediately be reported and will be exempted from the right of use. In addition to this, students could be able to meet the teacher online via our app. During the pandemic situations, we saw that compared to student studying at university, school children had more difficulties in terms of lack of such platforms. Thus, this app will help them to get easily adapted with their learning process.
